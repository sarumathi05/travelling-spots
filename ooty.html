Ooty Tourist Spots

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak is the highest peak in the Nilgiri mountain range and offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.

Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is a picturesque lake surrounded by eucalyptus trees and is popular for boating.

Botanical Garden

Botanical Garden is a beautiful garden with a wide variety of plants and flowers, including a fossilized tree trunk that is said to be around 20 million years old.

Rose Garden

Rose Garden is a beautiful garden with over 20,000 varieties of roses.

Pykara Falls

Pykara Falls is a scenic waterfall that is surrounded by lush greenery.

Avalanche Lake

Avalanche Lake is a beautiful lake surrounded by a dense forest and is a popular spot for trekking.

St. Stephen's Church

St. Stephen's Church is a historic church built in the 19th century and is known for its beautiful stained glass windows.

Government Museum

Government Museum is a museum that showcases the history and culture of the region.

Tea Museum

Tea Museum is a museum that showcases the history and process of tea production in the region.
